What Is the Safehouse Movement?

The Safehouse movement is a non-profit organization that shelters orphans, halts human trafficing, and help those in extreme strife in south america. Unlike anyother charities out there SafeHouse Movement, Inc. has never used $0.01 of donated money to operational and/or overhead costs. ALL donated money has gone to the actual cause. SafeHouse has no paid employees or contractors.
